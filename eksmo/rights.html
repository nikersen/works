Welcome to our foreign rights page!
Eksmo Non-Fiction being a part of the leading Publishing Group in Russia is recognized for high standard content that comes in a variety of forms across non-fiction. We offer extensive range of titles in well-being, esoterics, fashion and style, psychology, medicine, parenting, cookery, religion, art and many more from the most prominent and emerging Russian authors. Some of our authors are well-known worldwide.
Every third non-fiction book in Russia is published by Eksmo. The books by our authors are translated into many languages of the world and can be found in the bookshops of European and Asian countries. Eksmo Non-Fiction implements the brightest book and media projects, promotes successful international brands in the market and discovers new authors. Eksmo Non-Fiction introduces the best national and foreign non-fiction books to the Russian readers and pays huge attention to the integration of the book business in different types of content and to its maximum universalization.
